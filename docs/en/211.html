Call 2-1-1

2-1-1 is a free, confidential phone line operated 24/7/365 by United Way of Metro Chicago. Trained specialists connect you to health and social services across Cook County.

2-1-1

Free. Confidential. 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

How to reach 2-1-1

📞 Call Dial 2-1-1 from any phone. Available in 150+ languages.
💬 Text Text your zip code to 898-211.
🌐 Online 211metrochicago.org
✉ Email info@211metrochicago.org

What 2-1-1 can help with

  • Food pantries and meal programs near you
  • Emergency and transitional housing
  • Utility and rent assistance programs
  • Healthcare, mental health, and substance use services
  • Childcare and early education programs
  • Employment and job training
  • Immigration and legal services
  • Domestic violence resources

What to know before you call

2-1-1 does not provide services directly — they connect you to organizations that do. Tell the specialist what documentation you have or don't have. You do not need to be a citizen or have a fixed address to use 2-1-1.

For city-specific services (pothole repair, housing violations, city programs), call 3-1-1 instead.

For mental health emergencies, call or text 9-8-8.

For life-threatening emergencies, call 9-1-1.
