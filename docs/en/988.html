9-8-8 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

9-8-8 is the national mental health crisis line. Free, confidential, available 24/7. You do not need to be suicidal to call — anyone experiencing a mental health crisis can reach out.

9-8-8

Call or text. Free. Confidential. 24/7.

Who it's for

  • Anyone experiencing a mental health crisis
  • People thinking about suicide or self-harm
  • People feeling overwhelmed, hopeless, or in emotional distress
  • Family members or friends concerned about someone else

What to expect

When you call or text 9-8-8, you'll be connected to a trained counselor. Calls are routed to a local crisis center when possible. The counselor will listen, provide support, and connect you to resources if needed.

Calls are confidential. Counselors are available in Spanish and many other languages.

Other crisis resources

NAMI Chicago Helpline: 833-NAMI-CHI (626-4244) — Monday–Friday 9am–5pm. Not a crisis line, but provides support, guidance, and referrals for mental health needs.

Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741.

For life-threatening emergencies: Call 9-1-1.
